Brief Introduction to RoadVisor

RoadVisor is an innovative augmented reality navigation application designed to enhance your driving experience. By combining real-time traffic information with advanced computer vision techniques, RoadVisor aims to provide a safer, more efficient, and enjoyable journey for drivers. Discover how our cutting-edge technology can improve your time on the road and make driving a pleasure.

Features

Enhance your driving experience with RoadVisor's cutting-edge technology.

Augmented Reality Navigation

RoadVisor provides an AR-based navigation system that overlays directions on the real-time camera view, making it easier for drivers to follow the route and stay focused on the road.

Traffic Light & Sign Detection

RoadVisor detects traffic lights and signs in real-time, providing drivers with important information about the road ahead, ensuring they remain aware and compliant with traffic regulations.

Pedestrian Detection

RoadVisor uses advanced computer vision techniques to detect pedestrians in the camera view, helping drivers stay aware of their surroundings and prevent accidents.

Emergency Assistance

RoadVisor enables users to quickly send SOS messages to designated contacts in case of emergencies or accidents.
